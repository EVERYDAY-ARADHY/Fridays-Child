About Her

Welcome to Friday's Child, an art sanctuary curated to showcase the work of Ms. Aditi. Within this space, you'll discover a collection of different creative expressions and her munchidi. The gallery features a wide variety of art styles, ranging from poetry and stories to visual edits and photography. Each piece is collected to inspire and offer a cozy retreat into a world of imagination and cutecore aesthetics.
